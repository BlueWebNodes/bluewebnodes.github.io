@PotsdamCentralSchoolMusicFriends

The PCS Music Friends group welcomes new members!

We are a group that formed to support the music programs in the Potsdam Central School (PCS) district. The goals of PCS Music Friends include advocating for a strong music program at PCS and fundraising to support extracurricular music events, NYSSMA, All-County and All-State music event fees, and other needs.

Membership is open to anyone who would like to help support the music programs at PCS. The Music Friends group meets about every two months, virtually. Feel free to join even if you feel that you will only have time to help occasionally. You may also opt to become a contributing member.
