New Design

WordPress W8 Website

It is an eCommerce website for restaurant reservations built with WordPress and WooCommerce. The customers visit the restaurant waitlist page by scanning the QR code. They can fill out the form to join the regular waitlist or purchase a FastPass to go faster. The customers will get SMS and Email notifications when it is their turn. The features include web forms, a shipping cart, a credit card payment gateway, etc.
